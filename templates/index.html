Compete

Present your project at the end of the hackathon to our panel of judges, and win various prizes! Grand prizes are available for the top three teams, the best overall, the best software, and the best hardware project. Additionally, smaller prizes will also be available for various categories.

FAQ

Who is coming?

We're expecting 200 hackers from all over New England to attend!

Who can attend?

All students are welcome! However, we cannot grant prizes to students under 18. They are still more than welcome to attend!

Will there be travel reimbursements?

Sadly, we cannot provide reimbursements for travel. The event, however, doesn't cost a thing!

What if I don't have experience?

Doesn't matter! We'll have plenty of mentors from sponsoring companies, as well as workshops throughout the weekend!

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, chargers, a sleeping bag, and whatever else you need to make yourself comfortable. Food is provided.

What if I don't have a team?

Doesn't matter! We'll have plenty of mentors from sponsoring companies, as well as workshops throughout the weekend!

For issues, please contact acm@wpi.edu
